Ready Resources

Songs, skits, ceremonies, and quick references for meetings and campfires.

    Ready Resources Library

    Keep this page open during planning meetings. It is organized by what leaders usually need on short notice.

    Songs

    Opening, action, and closing songs with simple prompts.

    • Opening: "Scout Vespers" and "America the Beautiful"
    • Action: Repeat-after-me songs and call-and-response chants
    • Closing: Quiet reflection songs for campfire wrap-up

    Skits

    Fast skits by group size and prep time.

    • 2-4 people: joke skits and prop skits
    • 5-8 people: audience-participation skits
    • No prep: one-minute fillers for transitions

    Ceremonies

    Leader-ready outlines for recurring ceremonies.

    • Flag ceremonies: opening and retiring scripts
    • Awards: rank advancement and special recognitions
    • Crossovers: simple script with role checklist

    Meeting Fillers

    Quick options when a segment ends early.

    • Gathering game prompts
    • Two-minute challenge ideas
    • Reflection questions for den or patrol circles

    How to Expand This Library

    1. Create subfolders like `songs/`, `skits/`, and `ceremonies/` under `tools/ready-resources/`.
    2. Add printable pages and link them from this index.
    3. Open each page once while online so service worker caching keeps it available offline.
